Transfer calculator

This service will help you calculate the transfer amount for each child based on the invoices from their childcare providers and your personal contribution balance.

What you will need

Before you start, make sure you have the following information:

  • Invoice amounts from provider 1 for each child
  • Invoice amounts from provider 2 for each child

Information we already have

We already know the following information:

  • Number of children with accounts
  • Their personal contribution balance

Using the information you provide, we will calculate:

  • Total invoice amount for each child
  • Amount to be transferred into each child's account
